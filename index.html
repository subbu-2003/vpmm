Rules & Regulations

Please read and follow all guidelines for a smooth technical meet experience.

  • Entries are invited from ALL Departments of Arts & Science colleges and Engineering Colleges.
  • Registrations ends on 5th NOV 2025.
  • Events are common to all UG and PG students.
  • Participation certificates will be given to ALL registered participants.
  • Participants must carry their college ID card for verification.
  • All participants should report at the registration venue before 8.40 AM.
  • Registration fees is Rs 200/- per candidate and should be paid through online.
  • Judges decisions will be final and binding.
  • The participants should take care of their belongings; college is not responsible for the same.
  • Organizers reserve the right to modify rules of events, cancel events, if need arises.
  • Participants should ensure that no event clashes with one another.
  • Any changes in the entries will have to be informed on or before 5th NOV 2025.
  • Lunch & Refreshments will be provided for ALL participants.
  • Any kind of misbehavior/indecency will not be allowed.
