As You Like It

A creative freestyle event where participants can showcase any skill they love — drawing, crafting, video editing, short film, poster design, animation, or any unique talent. The stage is yours. Present anything **as you like it**!

Rules & Regulations

  • This is an individual event.
  • Participants may perform **any creative activity** of their choice.
  • All materials or resources must be arranged by participants themselves.
  • Time limit: **3 to 5 minutes** per participant.
  • Offensive, copyrighted, or unsafe content is strictly prohibited.

Judging Criteria

  • Creativity & Originality
  • Presentation & Confidence
  • Audience Impact
  • Effort & Uniqueness
  • Time Management
